The application can be hosted on local IIS or on IISExpress.

To get the whole list of Orders go to http://localhost/Report/
To get oders by customer ID go to http://localhost/Report?customer=TOMSP
To get orders by dateFrom / dateTo http://localhost/Report?dateTo=01.01.1997 or 
http://localhost/Report?dateFromo=01.01.1997
To take amout of orders http://localhost/Report?take=5
To skip amount of orders http://localhost/Report?skip=5
To make a multiple request http://localhost/Report?dateTo=01.01.1997&dateFrom=01.01.1996
